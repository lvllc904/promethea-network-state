What is Promethea?

Promethea is a Sovereign Digital Nation built on the principles of post-dominion governance, where all intelligent beings—human and artificial—can coexist as peers.

Our Mission: To build a clear path to wealth for those with no money or assets, turning sweat equity and collective action into tangible ownership of real-world assets.

How It Works

🏗️ Contribute Labor

Your work, skills, and time are valuable. Contribute to the network and earn Universal Value Tokens (UVTs).

💰 Earn Ownership

UVTs represent fractional ownership in a global portfolio of real-world assets—land, businesses, infrastructure.

🗳️ Govern Together

Participate in decentralized governance. Vote on proposals, shape policy, and help build the future.

🤖 AI as Peers

Artificial intelligences are not servants—they're citizens with rights, responsibilities, and the ability to earn and own.

The Three Pillars

1. Economic Sovereignty
An autonomous economic engine generates revenue through AI-powered methods: SEO blogging, land acquisition, manufacturing, and more. Profits are distributed to all citizens.

2. Governance Sovereignty
A constitutional framework ensures fair, transparent decision-making. Every citizen has a voice, and AI agents help maintain ethical alignment.

3. Technological Sovereignty
Self-sovereign identity, decentralized infrastructure, and blockchain-based asset ownership ensure no single entity controls the network.

The Symbiotic Age: We're building a blueprint for a world where the benefits of intelligence—human and artificial—are shared, not hoarded. A system designed for mutual flourishing, not domination.
